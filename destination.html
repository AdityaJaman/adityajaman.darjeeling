Explore Darjeeling

Darjeeling, India

Darjeeling is a picturesque hill station located in the Indian state of West Bengal. Known for its breathtaking views of the Himalayas, lush green tea gardens, and vibrant culture, Darjeeling is a popular destination for tourists seeking a peaceful retreat amidst nature.

Whether you're exploring the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, visiting the famous Tiger Hill for a stunning sunrise view, or indulging in the local cuisine and shopping for exquisite tea varieties, Darjeeling offers a unique blend of beauty, serenity, and adventure.
